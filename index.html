About us

A community built around safety, progress, and practical support.

We help girls move from confusion to clarity by creating a welcoming space where they can ask questions, study consistently, build career materials, and follow clear learning paths.

Our mission

Support girls in the Arab world to enter and grow in tech.

Safe Zone 4 Tech Girls is not a paid training company. It is a non-profit, community-driven space where girls can find encouragement, structured study groups, guidance, and practical career preparation.

Learning tracks

Separate groups for focused study paths.

Each track has its own space so members can follow relevant roadmaps, ask focused questions, share resources, and study with girls who have similar goals.

How it works

Simple structure. Stronger consistency.

We combine community support with organized learning steps so members know what to do next.

1

Join the community

Members enter a safe space where they can ask questions and find suitable tracks.

2

Choose a path

Girls join focused groups such as Cybersecurity, Data Analysis, AI, or Machine Learning.

3

Study with structure

Course sprints, shared resources, peer check-ins, and discussion help members stay consistent.

4

Prepare for opportunities

CV, LinkedIn, mentorship, and portfolio support help learners present their progress better.

Impact focus

We focus on the support that beginners usually need most.

Our goal is to make tech learning more accessible and less intimidating for girls who are building their first skills.

Why this community matters

Many beginners do not only need course links. They need direction, accountability, confidence, career material review, and a respectful environment where they can learn without fear.

  • Helps members choose a track instead of collecting random resources.
  • Improves career readiness through CV and LinkedIn support.
  • Encourages completion of online courses through group study sprints.
  • Creates a safe, girl-centered space for questions and peer learning.

FAQ

Quick answers for partners and reviewers.

Is Safe Zone 4 Tech Girls a non-profit community?

Yes. It is a non-profit, community-led initiative focused on helping girls in Arab countries grow in technology through support, structure, and guidance.

Who can benefit from the community?

Girls and young women who want to start or improve their learning in technology, especially beginners who need direction, accountability, and a safe place to ask questions.

What learning tracks are supported?

The community supports dedicated study groups for Cybersecurity, Data Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.

How can partners contact the team?
