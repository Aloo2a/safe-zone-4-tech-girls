About us

A safe zone for learning, confidence, and career growth.

We organize practical support for girls who want to explore technology, build strong profiles, and study consistently with peers.

Our mission

To make technology learning more accessible for girls in Arab countries through community support, structured study groups, mentorship, and practical career-readiness activities.

SafeSupport without judgment.
PracticalReal learning steps and tasks.
InclusiveOpen to beginners and learners.

What makes us different

Instead of leaving learners alone with long roadmaps, we divide the journey into small actions: profile reviews, CV help, track groups, guided course sprints, and peer encouragement.

Peer-ledGirls learn with girls.
Track-basedEach field has its own space.
Career-awareLearning plus employability.

Impact model

How we move a learner from confusion to action.

Our support model connects learning, career readiness, and community encouragement.

Access + Structure + Support

We help girls who may feel lost at the beginning of their tech journey by giving them a safe place to ask, learn, practice, and improve their professional presence.

1

Choose a track

Members identify the technology field that matches their current interest and skill level.

2

Join a focused group

They study with learners in the same path instead of following random scattered resources.

3

Study with accountability

edX courses and resources become easier through shared check-ins and peer discussion.

4

Build a professional profile

CV, LinkedIn, and portfolio support helps members present their progress clearly.
